The Challenge

The Cost of Managing Credit the Old Way

Nigerian lenders are managing complex credit processes with tools that were never designed for the job — and it is costing them in NPLs, regulatory exposure, and lost business.

📋

Fragmented Processes

Email approvals, Excel credit memos, WhatsApp sign-offs. No single source of truth. Broken audit trails. Version control failures at every stage.

⚖️

Inconsistent Credit Decisions

Without a structured framework, quality depends on the individual — not the institution. Subjectivity and unquantified risk enter the portfolio unchecked.

🏛️

Regulatory Exposure

CBN's Consumer Protection Regulations, prudential guidelines, and bureau mandates place specific obligations on every lender. Many cannot demonstrate compliance during examinations.

🐌

Slow Turnaround Times

Manual handoffs between departments introduce delays at every stage. In a competitive market, slow credit decisions drive clients to faster, more responsive lenders.

🔓

Weak Post-Disbursement Controls

Conditions Precedent waived without authorisation. Conditions Subsequent tracked on spreadsheets or not at all. Undisclosed risk that surfaces only at default.

📉

No Management Visibility

No real-time view of the loan pipeline — what is pending, what is overdue, where bottlenecks form, or what the aggregate credit exposure looks like.

⚖️

The Regulatory Reality — What CBN Requires

Credit bureau check before every approval — documented
Key Facts Statement (KFS) acknowledged before signing
Four-eyes approval on all credit decisions — audit trail required
Complete credit file retained per facility
Committee governance documented for large exposures
All Conditions Precedent verified before disbursement

Regulatory Alignment

Built Around CBN Requirements

CRMS treats regulatory compliance as a design constraint — not an afterthought. Every requirement is enforced at the system level, not left to individual discipline.

🏦 CBN Consumer Protection (2022)

KFS auto-generated at point of offer. Operations Officer must confirm KFS was presented and acknowledged by the customer before acceptance can be recorded. Confirmation is stored permanently.

📋 Credit Bureau Mandate

Bureau checks enforced as a mandatory step in Credit Analysis — not optional, not skippable. Results retained permanently and available for examination at any time. Covers borrower entity, all directors, all guarantors.

✋ Four-Eyes / Maker-Checker

Applied to credit scoring parameters, collateral valuations, CP waivers, and CS extension approvals. The proposing officer cannot approve their own change. Dual authorisation is enforced at the system level.

🗳️ Committee Documentation

Every vote, every rationale, every decision is on record. Quorum is tracked in real time. Overdue committees can be formally closed with a documented basis. No more minutes written after the fact.

📖 Immutable Audit Trail

Every action — approval, rejection, vote, document upload, bureau check, parameter change — is recorded with actor identity, role, timestamp, and comments. The trail cannot be edited or deleted.

✅ Disbursement Controls

Conditions Precedent must be formally satisfied or waived before disbursement. Waivers require Risk Manager approval. The system physically blocks disbursement until all mandatory CPs are resolved.

Architecture Principles

🏗️
Clean Architecture

Business logic is fully isolated from infrastructure. Database engines, storage providers, and external APIs are swappable without touching core application code.

⚡
CQRS Pattern

Commands (state changes) and Queries (reads) are fully separated — giving independent optimisation, clear intent, and simplified compliance auditing.

🧠
Domain-Driven Design

Core business rules are enforced at the domain layer — invalid states are impossible at the code level, not just the UI. Your data integrity is structural.

🔌
Interface-Driven Integrations

Bureau providers, core banking connectors, and open banking APIs are abstracted behind interfaces — new providers integrate without application changes.

Role guards applied at both the UI (what users see) and the application command layer (what they can execute) — URL manipulation cannot bypass access controls.

Each institution receives a dedicated deployment. No shared tenancy. Your data is never co-mingled with another institution's data under any circumstance.
